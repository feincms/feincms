FeinCMS 1.13 release notes
==========================

Welcome to FeinCMS 1.13!


Compatibility with Django 1.10
==============================

The biggest feature of FeinCMS 1.13 is being compatible with Django 1.10
and Django 1.11.

==============================

* None.

------------------------------

* None.

* None.

Bugfixes

* Too many to list.
